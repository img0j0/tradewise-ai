Help & Support

Getting Started

How do I search for stocks?
Use the search bar at the bottom of the main page to search for any stock by symbol (like AAPL) or company name (like Apple). Our AI will provide intelligent analysis and recommendations.
What are the dropdown sections for?
The three dropdown sections (Popular Stocks, Investment Themes, Market Insights) provide quick access to trending stocks, investment categories, and live market analysis to help you discover investment opportunities.

AI Features

How accurate are the AI recommendations?
Our AI analysis provides recommendations based on technical indicators, market trends, and historical data. While our models are sophisticated, all investments carry risk and you should always do your own research.
What does the confidence score mean?
The confidence score shows how certain our AI model is about its recommendation, ranging from 0-100%. Higher scores indicate stronger conviction in the analysis.

Account & Trading

How do I access my portfolio?
Click the tools icon (⚙️) in the top right corner and select "Portfolio" to view your holdings, performance, and trading history.
Is my data secure?
Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your personal and financial information. We never share your data with third parties without your consent.
